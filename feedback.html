Distillery is a space shaped by the people who enter it.

We want our club to be a place where people can feel welcome, respected, and free. Still, not everything that happens on a night reaches us directly. Some moments remain unseen, unheard, or unspoken.

If something made you feel uncomfortable, unsafe, excluded, or if you experienced or witnessed behaviour that should not be part of our space, please let us know.

We also welcome feedback about moments that felt good, things that worked well, or situations in which you felt supported. These perspectives help us understand what we are doing right — and where we need to do better.

All messages will be treated with care and respect.

If you would like us to reply, you can leave your email address here. This is optional.

Before the message is sent, you may be asked to complete a spam protection check. This form currently uses an external form service to forward messages by email. Later we can replace this with the official Distillery awareness address and a self-hosted endpoint.
